Govt-dealers agreement

Facebook Count

MULTAN, Dec 11: Petrol pumps in south Punjab reopened after a three-day closure on Wednesday night.

A meeting between District Coordination Officer Zahid Saleem Gondal and representatives of the All-Pakistan Petroleum Dealers Association was held in the evening and an agreement was reached to resume fuel supply.

The APPDA agreed to open the filling stations till Dec 31 as the DCO assured them of sympathetic hearing. — Staff Correspondent
